Layout & Polish

Two-column division grid — AFC on the left, NFC on the right (or 2 divisions per row on wide screens). Easy win for the blank space.
Collapsible divisions — expand/collapse to reduce scrolling once predictions are set
Quick-view tooltips — hover a team to see their game-by-game picks without opening the modal

New Features

Export/Share — generate a shareable image or URL of your full bracket (great for group competitions)
Conference record tracking — auto-compute each team's record vs AFC/NFC opponents from game picks
"Who beats who" matrix — a small grid per division showing head-to-head matchup picks at a glance
Compare mode — import a friend's predictions and diff them against yours

Fun/Analytics

Season narrative — auto-generate a text summary of your predicted season ("The Bills go 14-3 and clinch the AFC East in Week 15...")
Historical comparison — show how your predicted record compares to each team's actual record over the past few seasons
